RIOS

RIOS was our first software that helped users predict the best place to invest for improvements to a water fund. The development cycle was memorable and includes memorable artifacts.

These crowns were hand crafted by local artists Adrian Vogl and Heather Tallis for James Douglass and Richard Sharp, the primary software developers of RIOS.

Halloweed

NatCap has participated in All Hallows' Eve since its founding. Activities have included trick-or-treating and guising, monitoring haunted attractions, as well as donning costumes modeled after supernatural figures like horses, bananas, and tacos.
